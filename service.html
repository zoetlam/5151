Our Services

Angel's Lashes - Where Your Beauty Is Enhanced!

Eyelash Extension

Classic Eyelash Extensions

Using the 1:1 technique, each individual lash fiber is meticulously applied to a single natural eyelash, resulting in a seamless and natural look. This service is ideal for clients blessed with abundant natural lashes, offering a subtle enhancement that beautifully accentuates your eyes.

Cashmere Eyelash Extension

An upgrade of the classic extension not only creates a mascara look for your eyes but also provides better retention. You will love its softness. You won't feel that you are wearing it because it is so light! Angel’s Lashes highly recommend this type of extension if you want a natural but still visible look.

Hybrid Eyelash Extension

Combining the best of both worlds, this unique style blends volume lashes with classic individual lashes, resulting in a captivating and more dramatic look. Experience the perfect fusion of fullness and intensity, your beauty is elevated to new heights.

Volume Eyelash Extension

Through skillful artistry, multiple strands of individual lashes are meticulously bound together, creating a full and dramatic effect that beautifully enhances your eyes as well as giving you voluminous lashes that exude glamour and confidence

Mega Volume Eyelash Extensions

A stunning fan of 10 - 16 extensions is delicately applied to each natural eyelash, creating a captivated look because of its incredible fullness and intensity. Unleash your inner diva and embrace the glamour of Mega Eyelash Extensions for an unforgettable and eye-catching appearance.

Customized Designed Eyelash Extension

Angel’s Lashes would love to tailor your own style by applying color or special styles of eyelash extensions in order to make your uniqueness shine and your beauty from within radiate.
