Our Services

Angel's Lashes - Where Your Beauty Is Enhanced!

Classic Full Set Eyelash Extensions

Using the 1:1 technique, each individual lash fiber is meticulously applied to a single natural eyelash, resulting in a seamless and natural look. This service is ideal for clients blessed with abundant natural lashes, offering a subtle enhancement that beautifully accentuates your eyes. Experience the artistry of our lash experts and embrace a more effortlessly glamorous appearance with our Classic Full Set Eyelash Extensions.

Volume Full Set Eyelashes Extensions

Through skillful artistry, multiple strands of individual lashes are meticulously bound together, creating a full and dramatic effect that beautifully enhances your eyes. Let our talented technicians work their magic, giving you voluminous lashes that exude glamour and confidence. Experience the transformative power of our Volume Full Set and embrace a captivating new look.

Hybrid Volume Full Set Lash Extensions

Combining the best of both worlds, this unique style blends volume lashes with classic individual lashes, resulting in a captivating and more dramatic look. Experience the perfect fusion of fullness and intensity as our skilled technicians create a set of lashes that will elevate your beauty to new heights. Embrace the allure of our Hybrid Volume Full Set and unveil a truly stunning and glamorous appearance.

Mega Full Set Lash Extensions

With the Mega Volume technique, a stunning fan of 6-16 extensions is delicately applied to each natural eyelash, creating a truly show-stopping look. Prepare to be captivated by the incredible fullness and intensity that our skilled technicians can achieve. Unleash your inner diva and embrace the glamour of our Mega Full Set Lash Extensions for an unforgettable and eye-catching appearance.

Lash Refill

Our skilled stylists will replace missing extensions, remove outgrown ones, and ensure your lashes stay full and flawless. For best results, schedule your refill within 1-3 weeks with at least 50% of extensions remaining. Achieve lasting beauty or opt for a full-set transformation.

Lash (Perm) Lift

Experience enhanced curl and definition without the need for extensions. This gentle and natural alternative can provide stunning results that last for up to 6 weeks, ensuring your lashes look effortlessly captivating and beautifully lifted.

Lash Lift and Tinting

Elevate your natural lashes with our lash lift and tint service. Experience up to 6 weeks of stunning, long-lasting curl, followed by a nourishing Keratin treatment to strengthen and enhance your lashes. Complete the transformation with a customized tint, adding beautiful color, definition, and a brightening effect to your eyes.

Waxing

Achieve beautifully smooth and groomed skin with our waxing service. Unwanted hairs are expertly removed, leaving your skin irresistibly silky and flawlessly maintained. Experience the confidence and comfort of hair-free skin, courtesy of our professional waxing techniques. Unveil a touchably soft and polished appearance with our exceptional waxing service.
