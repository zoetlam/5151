Angel's Lashes - Where Your Beauty Is Enhanced!

What is eyelash extensions service?
expand_more
It is a beauty enhancement where synthetic fibers are individually attached to your natural eyelashes using a specialized adhesive. This process adds length, volume, and sometimes a touch of glamour to your lashes, giving you a lush, mascara-free look that can last several weeks with proper care.
How to keep eyelash extensions last longer?
expand_more
  • Use oil free makeup-remover. Steer clear of oil-based products near your eyes to protect the adhesive.
  • Clean your eyelash extensions with gentle lash foam cleanser when you wash your face.
  • Be gentle, avoid rubbing or pulling the extensions
  • Brush your lashes daily with a clean spoolie to keep them tidy and separate.
  • Resist the temptation to curl your extensions or use mascara, as it can compromise their longevity.
  • Consider touch-up appointments to maintain the fullness of your lashes to keep them looking fabulous.
